"Hi I'm Matt & i love Sound Design"

Welcome to my website, here I'm going to share with you my creative aspiration in making sounds from other worlds.

Music technology has inspired me to experiment into the layers of complexity, that is what makes so many sound instruments have a unique character despite being of digital nature.

I'm an avid user of a Virus TI, the oscillators and filters in this instrument sound amazing. Reaktor on the other hand i like to use as a modular effects unit and sequencing control of external instruments.

I enjoy learning Max by Cycling '74 and want to further develop my own projects with the visual programming language. Csound is also a complex sound language that can be anything you want it to be.
