Reaktor

One of the most renowned sound artist's that use Reaktor goes by the name of Richard Divine. His style is mostly glitch based compositions using modular arrangements of CV processors.

Recently in Reaktor 6, they have updated the software with modular in mind since the market has gone back like most things do. The content library is quiet impressive, displaying just how good Reaktor's instruments operate and also sound.

I also like to use my I-Pad using Lemur, it uses a UDP network protocol to send OSC messages. This enables more complex and dynamic control of parameters. Also Newscool generator is a cool x-y grid based sequencer based on cellular automata (GOL).
