Software that i like to use at the moment is Reaktor. It has a wide range of utility objects to construct instruments that seems more intuitive for audio processing.

Max on the other hand is more of a open language giving you more programming freedom to create thing from OpenGL, processing matrixes of data and being able to control data from external I/O.

Please have a look at both these visual programming languages for your creative interests.
